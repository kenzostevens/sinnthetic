Questions

Things you're probably wondering.

Will it sound like a robot talking to my customers?
+

No. It sounds like a professional, friendly receptionist, not Siri reading a Wikipedia article. We train it on your specific business details, your tone, and your services. Callers regularly do not realize they are talking to AI. You will hear every call transcript yourself so you can judge the quality firsthand.

What if the AI says something wrong about my business?
+

It will not. We build it specifically around your business, your services, your hours, your service area, and your pricing rules. It only says what it has been trained to say. It does not improvise or make things up. It never quotes specific repair prices to customers. It handles the booking and the details, and you handle pricing on the job.

If anything about your business changes, just text Kenzo and we update it within 24 hours at no extra cost.

What if a caller asks something the AI cannot answer?
+

It handles it gracefully. If a question comes up that is outside what the AI was trained on, it does not guess or fumble. It takes the caller’s name, number, and details, lets them know someone will follow up, and sends you an email summary so you can call them back personally.

Nothing falls through the cracks, and the caller never gets a bad experience.

My wife or office person already answers the phones. Do I still need this?
+

This does not replace her. It catches everything she cannot.

When she is on another call, the AI answers. When she goes to lunch, the AI answers. When she goes home at 5 PM, the AI keeps answering. Nights, weekends, holidays, sick days, the AI covers it all.

She does her job during her hours. The AI makes sure nothing slips through the cracks the rest of the time. Many contractors use both, a human during business hours and the AI for everything else. It is not either or.

What happens if I want to answer the call myself?
+

You are in complete control. We set it up however you want. Your phone can ring first with the AI as a safety net, or the AI can handle everything directly and send you a summary.

Your choice. And you can change it anytime.

Do I need to change my phone number?
+

Absolutely not. We work with your existing business line. It is a simple call forwarding setup. Your number stays exactly the same, and your customers never notice a change.

What if my business changes, new services, new hours?
+

Just tell us. We update your AI receptionist within 24 hours at no extra cost.

Add a service, change your hours, adjust your service area, it adapts as fast as your business does.
